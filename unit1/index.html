Rush: A Band That Changed the World

Early Beginnings

The Band rush started in the year 1968 with three band members. These band members included bass player Geddy Lee, guitarist Alex Lifeson, and drummer John Rutsey. It goes without saying, but it was a small band that they started in high school.


Neil Peart

Later on in the band's journey the drummer John Rutsey was replaced by Neil Peart, who is currently regarded by many as the best drummer in the world. The three of them then went on to become one of the best rock bands in the world. Here is a list of their top three songs:

  1. Tom Sawyer
  2. Spirit of the Radio
  3. Limelight
